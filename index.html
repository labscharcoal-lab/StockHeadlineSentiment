What is Stock Headline Sentiment?

Markets move fast, and filtering through daily financial news can feel overwhelming. Markets move on human emotion and narrative flow, making it easy to lose track of the crucial backstory behind sudden price movements. Stock Headline Sentiment acts as your deeply customizable, private trading diary that lives inside your Chrome Sidebar. This companion helps you construct an organic timeline of your ideas. It bridges the gap between reading web articles and organizing them into actual, usable insights. It passively watches out for tickers on your watchlist, analyzes headline data with the personal keyword list you build in your settings , and logs them cleanly into a personal diary without interrupting your reading routine.

The Philosophy: Map Price to Narrative

Have you ever stared at a sharp +/- percentage move on a chart, unsure exactly why it happened? Often, an asset moves because of structural trends or unexpected news breaks hitting an entire sector, rather than a headline about that specific asset. This journal is designed to build a personalized ledger over time, helping you visually bridge how price changes and macro-narratives correlate over weeks, months, and quarters.

🌍 Completely Asset & Market Agnostic

We purposefully do not tie this app to a single automated financial data provider. Because it relies purely on your custom text rules, you can use it to log and track any asset worldwide—whether it is US Equities, European Bonds, Crypto, Commodities, Forex, or niche international indices, completely independent of local currency.

🧘 Complete Strategy Privacy

Your specific market logic, unique keywords, and target watchlists belong to you alone. Every single entry, custom label, and data point is committed straight to your device's local memory—completely removed from third-party tracking servers.

🧠 The Power of Manual Review

Automated text parsing is a powerful speed booster, but it lacks human nuance. Since the engine relies on text matching, context can occasionally be misinterpreted. The tool places you in complete control, encouraging you to review, adjust sentiment labels, and enrich logs with manual context.

How it Works

Sidebar Features: Crafting Your Research Ledger

The sidebar serves as a hands-on workspace broken into simple, intentional sections:

  • 🏷️TAG Tab: Use this workspace to add tickers matching your ideas and interest. See how many headlines are tracked today.
  • 📚GROUP Tab: Grouping related tags under structural themes (e.g., "AI Trends", "Tech Titans") lets you track multi-asset industry sentiment cleanly, whenever required.
  • 📊 Stats Screen: A comprehensive visual dashboard showing cumulative data charts.
    Note: Visuals generated here can be exported as images for your research. Please be aware that as these charts reflect your private keyword configurations and logged data, any shared analysis is your sole responsibility.
  • ⚙️ Settings Dashboard (Your Phrase Engine): This is where you configure the text-matching rules. Input your specific watchlist codes, update targeted keyword strings for automatic headline entry, maintain data quality and easily run CSV data exports & import.

Everyday Tracking & Logging Guide

Capturing thoughts, processing events, and fine-tuning historical logs is built seamlessly into your workflow:

  • 🤖 Keyword-Driven Headline Auto Entry: Define specific positive or negative keywords in your settings. As you navigate financial boards, the extension automatically add headline with your tickers and sentiment that matches your keywords (🟢 Bullish / 🟡 Neutral / 🔴 Bearish) .
  • ✏️ Post-Log Review & Price Inputting: Open logged entries directly in the sidebar to review them for accuracy. Here, you can override mismatched automated colors, change tags, and manually append the asset price at the time of the event—ensuring your diary builds a highly accurate link between market price and market news.
  • 📝 Manual Content Entry: For the auto detected headline , select the ticker and sentiment button to add to your portfolio headline list.
  • 🖱️ Quick Right-Click Logging: Spot a piece of critical evidence inside a market article? Highlight the text, right-click to view Chrome's standard context menu, select 'Add Headline to Stock Headline Sentiment 'and send it to your sidebar journal with one click and add by selecting ticker and sentiment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why doesn't the extension fetch live asset prices automatically?

To make this software truly limitless, we explicitly avoided linking it to restrictive, regional asset price feeds. By entering asset prices manually, you can journal any financial tracking medium globally—whether it's alternative investments, international instruments, local currencies, or digital assets—without boundaries or paywalls.

Why did a headline get flagged with the wrong sentiment color?

The extension uses efficient literal text-matching rules based directly on the text keyword you define in Settings. It does not look at overall contextual logic, meaning sarcastic remarks or complex financial expressions might log incorrectly at first. This is why reviewing entries manually and correcting scores inside the sidebar is a vital step in maintaining your journal's historical value.

Is my trading journal private?

Completely. We don't host external processing clouds, database layers, or tracking trackers. Your watchlists, entry text, and manually input asset prices never leave your physical browser workspace.

Can I extract my data out of the system?

Yes. You can instantly export your compiled research into standard spreadsheet-friendly CSV tables at any point from the Settings screen.

Does this work on all financial websites?

The extension is designed to be site-agnostic. As long as the news content is displayed as text on a webpage, the extension can monitor it.Some website are complex to parse and may not be detected by engine .In those case right click and manual option can be used.

What if the extension stops tracking?

If the sidebar stops updating, a simple page refresh (F5) will re-establish the connection. See our Support page for a full troubleshooting guide.
